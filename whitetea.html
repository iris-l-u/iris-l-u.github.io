White Tea

White tea is created from the new buds and young leaves of the Carmellia Sinensis plant, and minimally processed — making it one of the most delicate tea types. The buds are harvested right before the oxidation process can begin, giving the tea’s white hue.

This style of processing, before oxidation, gives White tea its light, delicate, and fruity flavor. It is also has significantly lower caffeine levels compared to other tea types.
