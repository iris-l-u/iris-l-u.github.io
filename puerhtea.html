Pu-Erh Tea

Pu-erh, a fermented tea, is often more expensive than other tea types due to the length fermentation process it undergoes.

It is derived from the leaves and stems of the Carmellia Sinensis plant, and undergoes an additional aging process in a humid environment once harvested. This process brings out the tea’s distinctly dark and rich flavor, which is generally less acidic or bitter compared to other teas.
