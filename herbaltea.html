Herbal Tea

Herbal teas (aka “tisanes”) aren’t technically “true” tea types, but they’re brewed and consumed in the same way.

They are the end product of infusing leaves, stems, bark, roots, or any portion of a plant in water to release its active ingredients. Steeping the herbs in hot water brings out the plants’ benefits, including natural flavoring and essential oils.
